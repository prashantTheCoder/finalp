Must Visited Destinations

Jalmandir

The Jal Mandir, or Water Temple, is a prominent Jain temple in Pawapuri, Bihar, India

The Great Buddha Statue

The Great Buddha statue (Daibutsu) is one of the popular stops on the Buddhist pilgrimage and tourist routes in Bodh Gaya, Bihar (India).

Kaimur Hill

The Kaimur Hills stretch from near Katangi in Madhya Pradesh to near Sasaram in Bihar.

Glass Bridge

The Rajgir Glass Bridge is a 200-foot-high, 85-foot-long, and 6-foot-wide glass skywalk in the Rajgir Forest of Bihar, India.

Rajgir Sports Club

Introduction

The Rajgir Sports Complex, located in Bihar, is a state-of-the-art facility that has become a landmark for sports development in the state. It was inaugurated on National Sports Day in 2024 and stands as a combined sports complex and university, a first-of-its-kind initiative in India. Built at a cost of approximately ₹740 crores, it offers infrastructure adhering to international standards across 25 indoor and outdoor sports, including hockey, football, wrestling, kabaddi, and swimming.
